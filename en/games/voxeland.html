What is Voxeland?

Voxeland is a Survival RPG Open World based on Voxels, with procedural world generation (terrain, structures, npcs, etc). You can play with your friends to share unique experiences in game, fight with monsters, epic bosses battles, and more!

Skills and Combat

Fight against enemies to increase your character's skill, you can fight with any type of enemy, epic battles against bosses and dungeons, you will impress everyone!
